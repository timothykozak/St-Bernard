RCIA 2020

Congratulations to our newest members.

Front row, left to right: Krista Schaad, Zack Loeber, David Dodd, Tonya Dodd, Maggie Williams

Congratulations to our RCIA candidates who came into full communion with the Catholic Church on the Vigil of Pentecost, May 30th: David Dodd, Tonya Dodd, Zack Loeber, Krista Schaad, Maggie Williams. The Rite of Christian Initiation of Adults (RCIA) is an in depth process of studying the teachings of the Catholic Church and discerning God's will in preparation for receiving the Sacraments of Initiation (Baptism, Eucharist and Confirmation). Starting in the fall, candidates take weekly classes where they have the opportunity to learn the Church's teachings, ask questions, share time with other candidates, and prepare themselves for embracing the Catholic faith. St. Bernard is proud to have our newest members as part of our faith community. May the Lord reward them with many blessings.
